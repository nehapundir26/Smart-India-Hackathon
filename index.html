Comprehensive Civic Issue Management

Advanced features designed to streamline civic reporting and resolution

AI-Powered Classification

Advanced AI automatically categorizes your photos - whether it's a pothole, broken streetlight, or garbage issue, we identify it instantly.

Precise Geotagging

Your exact location is automatically captured and attached to reports, ensuring authorities know exactly where to respond.

Real-time Status Tracking

Track your reports with multiple status levels - Pending, In Progress, and Resolved. Get instant updates on issue resolution.

Local Issue Aggregation

View issues reported by other citizens in your area. See which problems are most prevalent and get collective resolution updates.

Direct Authority Contact

Connect directly with local authorities for urgent issues. Get immediate assistance when you need it most.

Secure & Anonymous

Report issues anonymously or with your details. Your privacy is protected while ensuring accountability in the system.

How Sahaaya Works

Four simple steps to report and track civic issues

1

Report New Issue

Use the app to report any civic issue with one or more images of the issue along with the location geotag which will be automatically attached to the photo.

2

Check Previous Reports

Check previously reported issue's status and get real-time updates on resolution progress from local authorities.

3

Contact Local Authority

For urgent situations, contact local authority representatives directly through the app for immediate assistance.

4

Track Local Issues

Check other local issues reported by other users. These are segregated on the basis of volume of complaints of that specific issue.
