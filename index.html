Powerful Features for Smart Cities

Built with cutting-edge technology to make civic reporting effortless and effective

AI-Powered Classification

Advanced AI automatically categorizes your photos - whether it's a pothole, broken streetlight, or garbage issue, we identify it instantly.

Precise Geotagging

Your exact location is automatically captured and attached to reports, ensuring authorities know exactly where to respond.

Real-time Updates

Track your reports in real-time. Get notifications when issues are acknowledged, in progress, or resolved by authorities.

Secure & Anonymous

Report issues anonymously or with your details. Your privacy is protected while ensuring accountability in the system.

Analytics Dashboard

View comprehensive analytics on issue types, resolution times, and improvement trends across different areas.

Community Driven

Join a community of active citizens working together to improve their neighborhoods and cities across Jharkhand.

How It Works

Reporting an issue is as simple as 1-2-3

1

Capture the Issue

Take a clear photo of the civic issue you want to report. Our AI will automatically identify the type of problem.

2

Add Details

Provide additional context, severity level, and your location is automatically captured for precise reporting.

3

Submit & Track

Submit your report and track its progress. Get updates when authorities acknowledge and resolve the issue.
